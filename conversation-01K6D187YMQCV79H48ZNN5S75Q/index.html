Recommendations

  • Immediate: Assess Bot automation opportunities - current 99 recipes suggest early adoption that could be accelerated
  • Short-term: Expand ETL/ELT capabilities training to unlock more data integration use cases
  • Strategic: While iPaaS dominance shows strong automation maturity, consider capability diversification for resilience
  • Training focus: Target teams not using Bot/ETL capabilities to identify department-specific automation needs
